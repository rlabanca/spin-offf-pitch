# 06 — Comms Strategy

## Channels

- **Micro-influencers** — for both the box and the membership.
- **Guerrilla marketing** — in-person and contextual.
- **Collab posts on social media** — with people from the OFFF community who have their own audiences.
- **Podcast appearances** — Pep and the SPIN OFFF team on adjacent podcasts to talk about the project.
- **Press release** — at kick-off, at membership launch, and around OFFF Barcelona 2027.
- **OFFF customer base** — direct activation of the existing OFFF audience.

## Goals

| Horizon | Goal |
|---|---|
| **Kick-off** (M1, Jan 2027) | Newsletter launches; reach 1,000 followers across socials |
| **6-month vision** (by M6, Jun 2027) | First profitable month; 400 paying members |

## SMART objectives (pre-launch & launch)

| When | Objective |
|---|---|
| Before Dec 2026 | ≥30 IG posts on the new SPIN OFFF account, drawing on OFFF legacy content |
| Before Dec 2026 | Concept ready for **at least 3 months** of newsletters (3 artists locked in) |
| Before Dec 2026 | Website live and ready |
| By Jan 2027 | 1,000 followers across social channels |
| First 3 months (M1–M3) | Collab posts with OFFF every month |
| By March 2027 (M3) | 500 newsletter subscribers |
| By March 2027 (M3) | Membership plan + creator team announced |
| By April 2027 (M4) | Membership opens; ~70 founding members |

## Key messages

1. **Fight against samification.**
2. **Meaningful art and design to help fix this messy world.**

## KPIs / metrics

- **MRR** (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
- **Growth rate** (MoM and WoW for newsletter subs)
- **MRR churn** (members lost / total)
- **MRR expansion** (existing members spending more — credit usage, box upsell)
- **Conversion rate** (newsletter sub → member)
- **CAC = €0 in Year 1** (no paid acquisition assumed)

## Target audience for comms

- **Primary:** OFFF's existing community — past attendees, social followers, partner-network audiences.
- **Secondary:** the broader SAM — *It's Nice That*-style audience reachable via featured-artist co-promotion and micro-influencer affiliate.

See [`02-market-opportunity.md`](02-market-opportunity.md) for sizing.
