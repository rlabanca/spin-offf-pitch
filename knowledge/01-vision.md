# 01 — Vision

## What SPIN OFFF is

A media company and new revenue stream for **OFFF Barcelona**, the world's leading creative-culture festival. Built on top of OFFF's existing community, artist relationships and sponsor network. Independent legal entity (Spanish S.L.).

## Why this exists — the problem we're addressing

| For whom | Pain |
|---|---|
| Artists | Need more **visibility, engagement, and new revenue streams** |
| Creative people | Need **connection and inspiration** in an increasingly disconnected feed |
| The creative world at large | Needs to **fight back against AI-driven "samification"** — the creeping homogenization of design, motion, illustration |
| Anyone who cares about culture | Needs a place where **DIY**, meaningful art and design help **fix this messy world** |

## Mission

Spread creativity, design and meaningful art around the world by amplifying the artists OFFF discovers — while giving them visibility, revenue and a community that buys their work.

## Vision

To be the global home of creative culture between OFFF festivals — a year-round media ecosystem where artists, designers and the curious meet.

## Key messages (the two we lead with)

1. **Fight against samification.** AI is making everything look the same. SPIN OFFF curates the opposite.
2. **Meaningful art and design to help fix this messy world.**

## Core narrative

> *We don't monetize until we've earned trust.*

For the first **three months** the newsletter is free. Membership only opens after the audience has been built and the value has been proven. This is non-negotiable — it's the brand promise.

## The "why now"

- AI homogenization is accelerating; differentiated creative voices are scarce.
- DIY and craft are culturally rising again.
- OFFF's community is reachable today (200K–400K people across past attendees, social followers, partner networks) — no ad spend required for launch.
- The 2027 OFFF Barcelona edition (May 6–8, 2027) is a built-in marquee moment for the membership funnel.
