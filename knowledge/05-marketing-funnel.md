# 05 — Marketing Funnel (TOFU / MOFU / BOFU)

## TOFU — Awareness

**Goal:** Make OFFF's existing audience subscribe to the newsletter. Sub-goal: create **more awareness about OFFF's legacy and community** (year-round, between festivals).

| Channel | Content |
|---|---|
| Social media (IG, TikTok) | OFFF stock / assets / legacy content + UGC + community moments — content OFFF wouldn't post as a brand (less curated, more human) |
| Website | Landing pages for each artist of the month |
| Podcast | Monthly artist interviews (long-form trust-builder) |
| OFFF community content | Cross-promotion through OFFF channels |

**Editorial rule:** TOFU content is the **less-curated** side of OFFF — UGC, behind-the-scenes, community moments. Things OFFF the festival brand wouldn't post itself.

## MOFU — Consideration

**Goal:** Convert newsletter subscribers into paying members.

| Channel | Content |
|---|---|
| Newsletter (the conversion engine) | Concept from artist of the month, curated detailed content |
| Website ads / social media | Membership-benefits content |
| Podcast | Member-perk callouts |
| **Micro-influencer affiliate program** | Activate community members with audiences |

**Content mix:** UGC + community + curated artist-of-the-month deep-dives. Each issue lands a soft membership CTA.

## BOFU — Conversion

**Goals (in priority order):**
1. Make subscribers **purchase the creative box** (Y2+).
2. Make paying members **spend their credits** in the OFFF marketplace ecosystem.
3. Make people **create content (UGC)** that becomes top-of-funnel for the next cohort.

**Content:** clear benefits-of-membership content, ecosystem stories (members redeeming perks, attending events), UGC competition mechanics that route back into TOFU.

## Channels at launch (consolidated)

- **Owned:** newsletter (Beehiiv), website, podcast (Spotify/Apple), YouTube, IG, TikTok.
- **Earned:** OFFF community cross-promotion, artist co-promotion, press coverage.
- **Affiliate:** micro-influencer program for box & membership.
- **Guerrilla:** in-person activations around OFFF Barcelona May 2027 + Sevilla + Montreal.

## What we will *not* do in Year 1

- **No paid acquisition** — growth comes from OFFF's network and artist co-promotion.
- **No FOMO-driven copy** — see [`09-brand-and-tone.md`](09-brand-and-tone.md). FOMO is explicitly forbidden.
