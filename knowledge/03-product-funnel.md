# 03 — Product Funnel

The full SPIN OFFF product is a **funnel of escalating commitment** built on a single creative concept per month (the "artist of the month").

```
          Website / Social / Podcast / OFFF community
                          │
                          ▼
                ┌─────────────────────┐
                │   FREE NEWSLETTER   │  ← TOFU (M1+)
                └─────────────────────┘
                          │
                          ▼
                ┌─────────────────────┐
                │    MEMBERSHIP       │  ← MOFU (M4+)
                │     €12 / month     │
                └─────────────────────┘
                          │
                          ▼
                ┌─────────────────────┐
                │  CREATIVE BOX SUB   │  ← BOFU (Q1 Y2+)
                └─────────────────────┘
```

## Layer 1 — Free Newsletter (M1+, January 2027)

- **Frequency:** weekly.
- **Editorial spine:** every month features one **artist of the month** who is paid (€2,000) to create the monthly concept and inspiration direction.
- **Content per issue:** curated work, artist interview/podcast cuts, community spotlights, links to OFFF legacy content.
- **CTA in every issue:** *join the membership waitlist.*
- **Distribution:** Beehiiv (or equivalent) + IG/TikTok shorts driving signups.

## Layer 2 — Paid Membership (M4+, April 2027)

**€12 / month.** Member benefits stack OFFF's existing ecosystem:

- **Free toolkits / discounts** via OFFF sponsors.
- **Exclusive digital art** from featured artists.
- **Credit system** giving access to events, partner products, and OFFF marketplace.
- **Early access** to OFFF tickets and **early-bird prices**.
- **Free tickets** to meetups and exclusive events.
- **Mentorship / networking** access to the OFFF roster.

The membership is the engine — it converts trust into recurring revenue *and* spreads spend across the OFFF ecosystem.

## Layer 3 — Creative Box (Q1 2028, Year 2)

A physical monthly box of materials curated by the featured artist so the subscriber can build a creative project inspired by them. EU-only at launch.

- **Brand partnerships** for box content (e.g., Lego, Moleskine).
- **Yearly award:** subscribers create from the box, the most-upvoted winner presents at OFFF as the prize. Drives massive UGC.

## The unifying mechanic — Artist of the Month

One artist anchors **everything** in a given month:

- They create the newsletter concept.
- They sit for the monthly podcast interview.
- They drive social content (collab posts).
- (From Y2) They curate the creative box.
- They co-promote across their own audience.

This turns the artist into a **co-marketer**, makes content production lean, and ensures every issue has a clear voice and visual identity.

## The "exclusive content" question (Pep's main concern)

> *How do we monetize when so much creative content is free online?*

**Answer:** SPIN OFFF doesn't compete on volume of free content. It competes on:
1. **Curation** — OFFF-tier taste, applied weekly.
2. **Access** — physical events, early-bird tickets, mentor networking, marketplace credit.
3. **Belonging** — the collab section + UGC ecosystem turn members into participants, not consumers. (See `12-key-messages-and-objections.md`.)
