## OFFF assets SPIN OFFF leverages

This is the **unfair advantage** — what makes the SOM reachable without paid acquisition.

| OFFF asset | How SPIN OFFF uses it |
|---|---|
| **Community** (past attendees, social followers — 200K–400K) | Direct launch audience for the newsletter |
| **Artist roster** (decades of OFFF speakers/featured artists) | Featured-artist-of-the-month pipeline |
| **Sponsor network** | Member perks (discounts/toolkits) and newsletter sponsors |
| **Government relationships** | Tax benefits via art-sponsorship structures |
| **Festival platforms** (Barcelona, Sevilla, Montreal) | Member benefits (early access, free meetups), guerrilla activation |
| **Brand equity & trust** | Warm-start credibility with creatives globally |
| **Design system / visual brand** | SPIN OFFF inherits and extends |

## How SPIN OFFF gives back to OFFF

- **Year-round attention** between festivals — keeps the OFFF brand top-of-mind.
- **New revenue stream** — diversifies OFFF's income beyond ticket sales and festival sponsorship.
- **Marketplace credit system** — funnels member spend into OFFF's partner ecosystem.
- **Audience growth** for OFFF events (early ticket access for members → higher conversion).
- **More UGC and content** featuring OFFF, OFFF speakers, OFFF community.
