## Phase 1 — Newsletter (M1–M3, Jan–Mar 2027)

**Goal:** Build the audience before asking for money.

| Month | Milestone |
|---|---|
| **M1 — Jan 2027** | Newsletter launches · target **1,000 subscribers** · 1,000 social followers · collab post with OFFF |
| **M2 — Feb 2027** | **2,500 subscribers** · first sponsor conversations · second collab post with OFFF |
| **M3 — Mar 2027** | **5,000 subscribers** · membership waitlist opens · membership platform built (€4K one-time) · first newsletter sponsor goes live (€500) · third collab post with OFFF |

## Phase 2 — Membership (M4–M12, Apr–Dec 2027)

**Goal:** Convert trust into recurring revenue. Membership opens **one month before OFFF Barcelona** (May 6–8, 2027) — this timing is intentional.

| Month | Milestone |
|---|---|
| **M4 — Apr 2027** | Membership launches · **150 paying members** · early-adopter pricing |
| **M5 — May 2027** | **OFFF Barcelona 2027** (May 6–8) — guerrilla activation, in-person member onboarding, press release #2 |
| **M6 — Jun 2027** | **400 members** · **first profitable month** · ad revenue starts (€200) · podcast/YouTube sponsorship begins |
| M7–M10 | Steady-state growth — newsletter sponsorship scales, MRR climbs |
| **M11 — Nov 2027** | **Cumulative break-even** |
| **M12 — Dec 2027** | **1,000 members · €16,500 MRR** · year-end report · planning for box launch |

## Critical-path dependencies

- **Featured artist pipeline** — without 3 confirmed artists by Dec 2026, M1 launch slips.
- **Membership platform** — must be built by end of M3. €4K budget allocated.
- **OFFF leadership alignment** — Pep + OFFF team buy-in is the gating factor (see internal pitch deck — priority #2).
- **First sponsor** — needed by M3 to start the €25K/Y1 newsletter sponsorship line.
