# 02 — Market Opportunity (TAM / SAM / SOM)

## TAM — Total Addressable Market
**~150–200 million people globally.**

The global audience interested in creativity, design, art, motion graphics, illustration and visual culture.

| Segment | Size |
|---|---|
| Creative professionals worldwide | ~20M |
| Design and art students | ~30M |
| Enthusiasts, hobbyists, adjacent professionals | 100M+ |

**Proxies:** Adobe Creative Cloud has ~33M subscribers. Behance has ~30M registered users. The broader interest group — people who follow design content on social media, attend creative events, or consume design media — extends well beyond professional users.

## SAM — Serviceable Addressable Market
**~15–20 million people globally.**

People specifically aligned with OFFF's world: professional designers, motion artists, creative directors, art directors, serious students, digital artists who follow cutting-edge international creative culture. Audience profile of platforms like *It's Nice That*, *Motionographer*, *Dribbble*, and *Dezeen* — highly engaged, internationally minded, willing to pay for quality content.

Defined **not by geography** but by **identity**: people who see creativity as both profession and lifestyle.

## SOM — Serviceable Obtainable Market
**~500K reachable. 10,000–30,000 paying members in the 3-year horizon.**

OFFF's existing community (past attendees, social followers, partner networks) gives SPIN OFFF a direct launchpad of **200,000–400,000 people** — reachable without paid acquisition.

| Horizon | Newsletter Subs | Paying Members | Estimated MRR |
|---|---|---|---|
| Year 1 | 5,000–10,000 | 300–1,000 | €3.6K–€12K |
| Year 2 | 25,000–50,000 | 2,000–5,000 | €24K–€60K |
| Year 3 | 75,000–100,000 | 8,000–15,000 | €96K–€180K |

At €12/month average membership, **10,000 members ≈ €1.44M ARR**.

## Target audience archetypes

- Creative professionals (designers, motion artists, illustrators, art directors).
- Serious art and design students.
- Digital creators and creative-adjacent professionals (creative tech, advertising, content).
- The OFFF worldwide community: past attendees, OFFF social followers, OFFF partner audiences.

## Why we can win this audience

- The OFFF brand is already trusted in this segment.
- The community is already curated — we don't need to build trust from zero.
- Our content is **co-created with featured artists** every month, who promote alongside us.
- No direct competitor combines: OFFF-tier curation + community + creative box + IRL festival access.
