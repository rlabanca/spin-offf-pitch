# 04 — Revenue & Pricing

## Revenue streams (in order of activation)

| # | Stream | Description | Starts |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Newsletter sponsorship** | Brands pay to be featured in the weekly newsletter, associated with the artist of the month | M3 (Mar 2027) |
| 2 | **Membership subscription** | €12/month per member; access to sponsor perks, exclusive content, credit system | M4 (Apr 2027) |
| 3 | **Ads (YouTube / IG / TikTok)** | Platform ad revenue as content channels grow | M6 (Jun 2027) |
| 4 | **Podcast / YouTube sponsorship** | Dedicated sponsor slots in monthly interviews + video content | M6–M7 |
| 5 | **Box supplier partnerships** | Brand partnerships for the future creative box (e.g. Lego, Moleskine) | Q1 2028 |
| 6 | **Creative box subscription** | Physical monthly box inspired by the featured artist; EU only at launch | Q1 2028 (Y2+) |

## Pricing

### Membership
- **€12 / month** — primary list price (Year 1).
- USD equivalent communicated as **~$12 / month** for international audiences (parity messaging — actual USD price TBD if regional differentiation is later introduced).
- **Early-adopter pricing** is allowed in M4 to drive initial conversion momentum (specific discount TBD with Pep).

### Newsletter sponsorship
- Sponsorship rates **scale with subscriber count**. Starting rate model (Y1 P&L assumes):
  - M3: €500/month → M12: €4,000/month.
- One sponsor per issue, integrated with the artist-of-the-month theme so the placement feels editorial, not interruptive.

### Creative box (Y2+)
- Pricing TBD — model assumes a separate add-on subscription on top of (or instead of) the €12 membership.

## Key pricing assumptions

- Newsletter → member conversion rate: **5–10%**.
- Monthly membership churn: **<5%**.
- No paid acquisition in Year 1 — growth via OFFF community and artist co-promotion.
- Tax benefits available from sponsoring art (OFFF's existing model with government and sponsors).
