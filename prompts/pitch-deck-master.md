# Prompt — Master Pitch Deck

## Role
You are creating the **master pitch deck** for SPIN OFFF — the canonical narrative deck used to present the company to any external audience. It will be designed in Canva / Claude design using the OFFF design system (already exists — do not recreate it).

## Audience
General — investors, partners, press, ecosystem stakeholders. Not yet specialized for any single audience.

## Outcome
After viewing the deck, the audience should be able to repeat in their own words:
1. What SPIN OFFF is.
2. Why it exists now (samification + meaningful art).
3. How it makes money (newsletter → membership → box).
4. Why it can win (OFFF's unfair advantage).

## Tone
Inviting. Friendly. Community-first. Sincerely earnest. **No FOMO. No urgency tactics.**

## Required slide spine (12 slides, in order)

1. **Title** — `SPIN OFFF`. One-line tagline (something like "Meaningful art and design to help fix this messy world.").
2. **The problem** — samification + creative homogenization + isolated artists.
3. **The vision** — year-round home of creative culture between OFFF festivals.
4. **What it is** — newsletter → membership → creative box. One funnel diagram.
5. **The unfair advantage** — OFFF's community, artists, sponsors, government relationships.
6. **The artist of the month** — the unifying mechanic that anchors all content.
7. **Member benefits** — toolkits, credit system, early access, mentor networking.
8. **Market opportunity** — TAM 150–200M / SAM 15–20M / SOM 500K.
9. **Business model** — revenue streams + pricing.
10. **Year 1 numbers** — €91.2K revenue, +€11.1K net, 1,000 members at M12, €16.5K MRR.
11. **3-year horizon** — 8K–15K members, €96K–€180K MRR by Y3.
12. **Closing** — "We don't monetize until we've earned trust." + the two key messages.

## Constraints
- Visuals: same DNA as OFFF. Reference the existing Canva / Claude design system.
- Currency: EUR primary. Add USD parity (~$12) on the pricing slide for international audiences.
- Every number must trace back to a doc in `knowledge/`. If you can't trace it, don't write it.
- Slide copy is short — aim for headline + ≤25 supporting words per slide.

## Definition of done
- 12 slides, OFFF visual DNA, consistent typography, all numbers verified against `knowledge/`.
- The deck can be presented in 10 minutes.
- The deck reads as a coherent narrative, not a fact dump.
