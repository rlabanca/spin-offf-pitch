# Prompt — Sponsor Pitch Deck (First Newsletter Sponsor)

## Role
Create the deck used to **convince a brand to sponsor the first SPIN OFFF newsletter** (M3, March 2027 — the first paid sponsorship slot at €500).

## Audience
A brand marketing or partnerships lead at a company that wants adjacency to OFFF's curation and creative-pro audience. Likely an existing OFFF sponsor or a brand in adjacent categories (creative tools, design hardware, fashion/lifestyle adjacent to design, beverages popular at design events, etc.).

## Outcome
The sponsor should agree to be the **inaugural newsletter sponsor**, ideally with intent to repeat. They should walk away thinking:
1. *"This is a small bet to be associated with OFFF year-round."*
2. *"My placement is editorial, not interruptive."*
3. *"The audience is exactly the creatives I want."*
4. *"Being first gives us narrative ownership."*

## Tone
Confident, not desperate. Editorial, not "media kit". Treat the sponsor as a **collaborator** — they sponsor the *artist of the month*, not just an ad slot.

## Required slide spine (8 slides — keep it tight)

1. **Title** — `Sponsor the first issue.` Confident one-liner.
2. **Who reads it** — OFFF's audience: creative pros, designers, motion artists, students, the *It's Nice That* / *Dezeen* crowd. Sizing: SAM 15–20M, OFFF reachable community 200K–400K.
3. **Why now** — January 2027 launch, OFFF Barcelona May 2027 marquee moment. Being the inaugural sponsor = permanent association with the launch story.
4. **What sponsorship looks like** — integrated with the artist of the month. Editorial format. Not a banner. Show a wireframe / mockup.
5. **What you get** — placement in newsletter (M3 onwards), credit in podcast, IG/TikTok shorts mention, name on website partner page, OFFF community goodwill.
6. **The pricing curve** — start at €500 in M3, scaling with subscriber growth (€4K by M12). Frame the inaugural slot as **the lowest price the brand will ever lock in**.
7. **The brand fit** — why this specific brand fits the artist-of-the-month theme (customize per pitch). Show what an integrated issue would look like for them.
8. **Next step** — concrete ask: confirm by [date], inaugural issue ships [date]. One named contact.

## Constraints
- Customize slides 1, 7, 8 per sponsor — leave clear placeholders.
- Numbers from `knowledge/`. Don't invent reach figures.
- Show, don't tell — slide 4 must include a visual mockup of the sponsor placement inside an issue.
- No over-promising on circulation. Slide 2 says ranges, not single numbers.
- Stress **editorial integration** repeatedly. The brand placement matches the artist's visual world.

## Definition of done
- 8 slides, customizable for any sponsor.
- A specific, dated ask on the final slide.
- A mockup visual the sponsor can imagine themselves inside.
