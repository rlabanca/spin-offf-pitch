# Prompt — Internal Pitch Deck (OFFF Team)

## Role
Create the deck used to **convince the OFFF Barcelona team** to back SPIN OFFF — actively support, share community access, allocate Pep's time, lend the brand.

## Audience
- **Primary:** Pep (already aligned in principle but needs the formal pitch to share with his team).
- **Secondary:** OFFF's marketing, ops, and community leads. They are the ones whose work changes if SPIN OFFF lands. They will resist if it feels like extra work or brand risk.

## Outcome
The OFFF team should leave thinking:
1. *"This makes OFFF stronger, not weaker."*
2. *"It doesn't add work to my plate — it adds revenue, attention, and audience."*
3. *"The financial risk is contained (€11K net Y1, break-even M11)."*
4. *"We retain creative control where it matters."*

## Tone
Collaborative. *"We"*, not *"I"*. Respectful of the OFFF brand and the team's existing workload.

## Required slide spine (10 slides)

1. **Title** — Why SPIN OFFF, why now, for OFFF.
2. **The strategic gap** — OFFF goes quiet between festivals. Audience attention drifts. Sponsors get less year-round value.
3. **What SPIN OFFF is** — newsletter → membership → box. Year-round oxygen for the OFFF brand.
4. **What OFFF gets back** — diversified revenue, year-round audience engagement, more UGC, stronger ticket pre-sales via member early access, marketplace-credit spend in OFFF's partner ecosystem.
5. **What it doesn't change** — OFFF the festival stays the festival. SPIN OFFF is a sibling, not a replacement.
6. **Decision rights & control** — Pep has final say on artists, sponsors, brand decisions. The OFFF team isn't asked to add operational work.
7. **The launch plan** — newsletter Jan 2027, membership Apr 2027, OFFF Barcelona May 2027 as the marquee moment.
8. **The numbers** — Y1 revenue €91.2K, costs €80.1K, net +€11.1K, 1,000 members at M12. Contained risk.
9. **Asking for** — a) Pep's time on key decisions, b) OFFF community-channel access for cross-promotion, c) sponsor-network introductions, d) brand-DNA usage.
10. **Closing** — the two key messages + "this makes OFFF stronger".

## Constraints
- Use OFFF visual DNA without ambiguity — this audience already knows the system.
- Avoid pitch-deck jargon ("synergies", "leverage", "category-defining"). Talk plainly.
- Pre-emptively address the cannibalization concern (slide 5). Don't wait for someone to raise it.
- One slide explicitly addresses **Pep's main concern** (free-content competition) — see `12-key-messages-and-objections.md`.

## Definition of done
- 10 slides, OFFF DNA, clear ask on slide 9.
- Can be walked through with the OFFF team in 15 minutes.
- Every team member can answer "what do I need to do differently?" with "very little — and here's what I get."
